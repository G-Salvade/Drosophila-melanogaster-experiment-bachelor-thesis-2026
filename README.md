# README - *Drosophila melanogaster* Bachelor thesis

### The influence of three chromosomal inversions on body size-related traits in *Drosophila melanogaster* from their ancestral range in Zambia

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Dataset: Morphological traits measurements

### Dataset overview

This dataset contains the measurements for the 10 karyotypes of *Drosophila melanogaster* regarding three specific traits: \* Wing area \* Femur length \* Tibia length

The dataset consists of a total of **6 documents**, as each morphological trait has been divided based on the sex of the flies.

### Files description

The 6 files correspond to the following structure:

1.  WFAM.csv: Wing area measurements for females
2.  WMAM.csv: Wing area measurements for males
3.  FFLM.csv: Femur length measurements for females
4.  FMLM.csv: Femur length measurements for males
5.  TFLM.csv: Tibia length measurements for females
6.  TMLM.csv: Tibia length measurements for males

## File Details

### 1.WFAM.csv

-   **Dimensions:** 221 rows × 3 columns
-   **Columns description:**
    -   **Karyotype:** Refers to the chromosomal arrangement (karyotype) of the analyzed fly.
    -   **Label:** Represents the name of each image, indicating the individual fly number and its replicate of origin.
    -   **Average:** Represents the average of the two measurements taken for each sample.

## Methods

### Morphological Measurements and Image Processing

All phenotypic traits were measured using **ImageJ** software. The measurements are structured and divided across the 6 data files based on the sex of the flies and the 10 analyzed karyotypes:

1.  **Female Wing Area:** The left wing area of 221 females of *Drosophila melanogaster* was measured and categorized according to the 10 analyzed karyotypes.

2.  **Male Wing Area:** The left wing area of 221 males of *Drosophila melanogaster* was measured and categorized according to the 10 analyzed karyotypes.

3.  **Female Femur Length:** The femur length of the first right leg of 220 females of *Drosophila melanogaster* was measured and categorized according to the 10 analyzed karyotypes.

4.  **Male Femur Length:** The femur length of the first right leg of 218 males of *Drosophila melanogaster* was measured and categorized according to the 10 analyzed karyotypes.

5.  **Female Tibia Length:** The tibia length of the first right leg of 220 females of *Drosophila melanogaster* was measured and categorized according to the 10 analyzed karyotypes.

6.  **Male Tibia Length:** The tibia length of the first right leg of 219 males of *Drosophila melanogaster* was measured and categorized according to the 10 analyzed karyotypes.
